Our Story

A Community of Learners & Leaders

Everest Academy was founded in 1999 with a single mission: to create an environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and personally. What began as a small institution with a handful of dedicated teachers has grown into one of the region's most respected schools.

We combine rigorous academics with holistic development, recognising that true education goes beyond textbooks. Our state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated faculty, and vibrant extracurricular programs ensure that students are prepared not just for examinations, but for life.

Today, we proudly serve over 2,400 students from Nursery through Grade 10, guided by a team of 45+ expert educators who are passionate about making a difference in every child's life.

Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To provide a high-quality, inclusive education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, builds character, and empowers every student to reach their fullest potential — academically, socially, and personally — so they may contribute meaningfully to society and the world.

🌟

Our Vision

To be the leading school in Nepal — recognised not only for academic excellence but for producing compassionate, innovative, and globally-minded graduates who are ready to lead and serve in the 21st century and beyond.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🎯

Academic Excellence

Consistently high results with personalised attention for every student. We set high standards and provide the support needed to meet them.

🤝

Character Building

We develop integrity, resilience, and compassion alongside academic skills — because who you are matters as much as what you know.

🌍

Global Perspective

Preparing students to be informed, engaged citizens of the world — curious about different cultures and ready to collaborate across borders.

🌱

Holistic Growth

Education of the whole child — mind, body, and spirit. Sports, arts, and wellbeing are as important to us as academic achievement.

💡

Innovation

We embrace modern teaching methodologies, technology, and creative thinking to keep our students ahead in a fast-changing world.

🏛️

Cultural Pride

Deeply rooted in Nepali culture and heritage, we celebrate who we are while preparing students for the global stage.
