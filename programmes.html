Our Programmes

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for a bright and purposeful future.

Academics

Academic Programmes

From early childhood to SEE preparation, our curriculum nurtures curiosity and builds excellence.

🧒

Pre-School Programme

A nurturing environment for early learners to develop foundational skills through play-based learning.

  • Montessori approach
  • Phonics & numeracy
  • Creative arts & music
  • Social development
📖

Primary Programme (1–5)

Building strong academic foundations while fostering curiosity, creativity and a love of learning.

  • Core subjects (Maths, English, Science)
  • Nepali & Social Studies
  • Computer literacy
  • Sports & arts
🔬

Secondary Programme (6–10)

Rigorous academic preparation for SEE examinations with personalised guidance and support.

  • SEE Board preparation
  • Optional subjects
  • Career counselling
  • Leadership activities
Enrichment

Co-Curricular Programmes

We believe in developing the whole student — beyond the classroom.

🎨

Arts & Culture

Dance, music, visual arts, and drama programmes that celebrate creativity and Nepali heritage.

  • Classical & modern dance
  • Visual arts studio
  • School orchestra
  • Annual cultural festival
⚽

Sports & Athletics

Comprehensive sports programme promoting teamwork, fitness, discipline, and healthy competition.

  • Football, cricket, basketball
  • Athletics & swimming
  • Yoga & meditation
  • Inter-school tournaments
💻

STEM & Technology

Hands-on learning in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics for 21st-century skills.

  • Robotics & coding
  • Science laboratory
  • Maths olympiad
  • Innovation challenge
